Albert Einstein

The best scientist in history

Here is a timeline of Albert Einstein life:

  • 1879 - Borns in Germany
  • 1869 - He began his higher studies at the Federal Institute of Technology in Zurich
  • 1901 - Acquire Swiss nationality
  • 1902 - Enter as an official at the Federal Patent Office in Bern
  • 1903 - He marries Mileva Maric, with whom he will have two children
  • 1905 - He publishes his first articles in which he deals with the fields of brownian motion, the photoelectric effect and special relativity
  • 1916 - Publishes the General Theory of Relativity
  • 1919 - Divorced from Mileva, he marries Elsa, a cousin of his
  • 1920 - He meets Leo Szilard, with whom he will develop inventions such as a and an electromagnetic pump with no moving parts
  • 1921 - Receives the Nobel Prize in Physics
  • 1939 - Letter to Roosevelt requesting him to undertake an atomic bomb research program
  • 1940 - Adopt American citizenship
  • 1945 - He retires from teaching to be able to exclusively develop his theoretical research
  • 1955 - Dies in Princeton (USA)
